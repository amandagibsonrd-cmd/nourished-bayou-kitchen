Services

Two separate ways to work together.

Virtual nutrition counseling and Nourished Bayou Kitchen workshops are two different services. Counseling is individualized virtual care, while kitchen workshops are small-group in-home experiences.

FAQ

Common questions.

Are all counseling sessions virtual? Yes. All one-on-one nutrition counseling sessions are offered virtually, so you do not need to attend in person for counseling support. Certain insurance plans are accepted for counseling sessions.
Do you offer anything in person? Yes. In-person services are reserved for Nourished Bayou Kitchen in-home workshops, which are scheduled separately after the discovery call.
What is the difference between counseling and a workshop? Counseling is individualized one-on-one virtual nutrition care. Workshops are separate small-group in-home cooking experiences that focus on food skills and practical nutrition education.
Who are the kitchen workshops best for? They work well for girls' nights, couples, small groups, wellness gatherings, and hosts who want a relaxed, memorable experience centered around food. They are optional and do not require ongoing counseling.
How do I book? Start by booking a discovery call through SimplePractice. From there, we can determine whether virtual counseling or a Nourished Bayou Kitchen workshop is the best fit.
