What to Expect

Every workshop is designed to feel relaxed, welcoming, and practical. You do not need to be an experienced cook to enjoy it.

A relaxed, hands-on experience

This is not a lecture. We cook together in a comfortable, real-life kitchen setting with plenty of room for questions and conversation.

Simple nutrition education woven into the cooking

As we prepare the meal, I share practical nutrition tips in a way that feels natural, helpful, and easy to apply at home.

Food that feels realistic and satisfying

The focus is always on meals that are approachable, flavorful, and supportive of everyday life, not perfection, dieting, or rigid food rules.

Guests leave with new cooking confidence, practical ideas, and recipes they can actually use again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you bring ingredients?

By default, the host provides groceries from a simple list. An ingredient add-on is also available if you would prefer for me to bring everything needed.

Is it beginner-friendly?

Yes. These workshops are relaxed, hands-on, and designed for real-life home cooks. No prior cooking experience is required.

Can you accommodate allergies or dietary needs?

Yes. Menus can be adjusted for common needs such as gluten-free, dairy-free, or vegetarian preferences.

Is this nutrition counseling?

This is cooking education led by a Registered Dietitian. It is practical, skill-based, and food-forward rather than one-on-one nutrition counseling.

Ready to host a cooking night?

Choose your theme, pick a date, and I’ll send a simple host checklist. You bring your people and we’ll make it practical, welcoming, and delicious.
