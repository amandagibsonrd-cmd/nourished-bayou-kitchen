Virtual counseling

One-on-one nutrition counseling that fits everyday life.

This is a supportive, individualized process focused on helping you create changes that feel sustainable. Whether you want more structure, better consistency, more confidence in your meals, or less overwhelm around food, counseling is tailored to your real life.

1

Start with your full picture

We look at your routine, eating patterns, barriers, preferences, and goals so your care plan is actually relevant to your life.

2

Build a realistic plan

Together we create practical strategies around meals, snacks, grocery habits, and daily rhythm without all-or-nothing pressure.

3

Adjust as life changes

Follow-up sessions help you troubleshoot, build consistency, and keep moving in a direction that feels sustainable over time.

FAQ

Common questions.

Are all counseling sessions virtual? Yes. All one-on-one nutrition counseling sessions are offered virtually.
Do you offer anything in person? Yes. In-person services are reserved for Nourished Bayou Kitchen in-home workshops, which are scheduled separately after the discovery call.
What is the difference between counseling and a workshop? Counseling is individualized one-on-one nutrition care. Workshops are small-group cooking experiences that focus on food skills and practical nutrition education.
Who are the kitchen workshops best for? They work well for girls' nights, couples, small groups, wellness gatherings, and hosts who want a relaxed, memorable experience centered around food.
How do I book? Start by booking a discovery call through SimplePractice. From there, we can determine whether virtual counseling or a Nourished Bayou Kitchen workshop is the best fit.
